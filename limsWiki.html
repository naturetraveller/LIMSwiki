LIMS Wiki

A Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) is a software solution designed to manage samples, data, and laboratory workflows efficiently.

Each of these systems has a different focus: LIMS is for data/sample management, ELN is for digital lab notebooks, and LES is for standardized workflows.

  • Sample Management: Tracks and organizes samples.
  • Data Integration: Connects with instruments and databases.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Supports FDA 21 CFR Part 11, ISO 17025.

Ensures compliance with GxP, ALCOA+, and other regulations in laboratory environments.

Top LIMS providers include LabWare, STARLIMS, LabVantage, and open-source alternatives like Bika LIMS.
