What is a LIMS?

A Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) is a software solution designed to manage samples, data, and laboratory workflows efficiently.

  • LIMS: Automates sample and data management.
  • ELN: Electronic lab notebooks for structured documentation.
  • LES: Standardized and automated laboratory workflows.
  • SDMS: Secure storage and management of large scientific datasets.
  • CDS: Chromatography data systems for precision analysis.
